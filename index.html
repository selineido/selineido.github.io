ABOUT ME


In the fall of 2016, during my first year of college at Algonquin, I took a course called Interactive Media Design. I've learned so much this coming year, and I love to learn new things every day.

This summer I got a job at Applied Research and Innovation at Algonquin College that has several research centers focusing on applying the latest knowledge and technologies in the creation of useful products, services, and processes.

The projects involve prototype development, feasibility studies, clinical trials, and technical consultation and market research for business, industry and community partners. I feel I am on the right pathway to continue developing my knowledge in learning when I finish college.
